Selected Projects

PhD Research

Mechanical Modeling of Fiber-Reinforced Emulsion Hydrogels

Developed theoretical and data-analysis tools to understand how droplet-fiber interactions and jamming transitions influence stiffness in soft composite materials.

  • Built mechanics-based models connecting microstructure to bulk modulus
  • Analyzed experiments across droplet volume fractions
  • Translated research results into predictive engineering insight

Image Analysis

Ellipse-Based Droplet Shape Analysis

Built a Python/OpenCV workflow for measuring droplet deformation from microscopy images and correcting geometric errors in ellipse-based fits.

  • Implemented automated droplet detection using fitEllipse
  • Developed correction methods based on circular-segment geometry
  • Improved robustness of shape measurements for deformed droplets

Computational Modeling

Composite Elastic Modulus Prediction

Created a MATLAB model to estimate the effective stiffness of composites containing polydisperse inclusions, with emphasis on structure-property relationships.

  • Explored modulus trends versus inclusion size distribution
  • Compared analytical approximations against numerical predictions
  • Framed results around engineering design tradeoffs
